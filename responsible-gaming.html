Signs of Problem Gaming

It's important to recognize the signs of problem gaming. You may need help if you:

  • Spend more time or money on gaming than you can afford
  • Feel restless or irritable when trying to stop gaming
  • Gamble to escape problems or relieve feelings of anxiety or depression
  • Chase losses by gambling more to win back money
  • Lie to family or friends about your gaming activities
  • Neglect work, school, or family responsibilities due to gaming
  • Feel the need to bet more money to get the same excitement

Tips for Responsible Gaming

  • Set a budget before you start playing and stick to it
  • Never chase your losses
  • Only play with money you can afford to lose
  • Take regular breaks from gaming
  • Don't play when you're upset, angry, or depressed
  • Balance gaming with other activities and hobbies
  • Keep track of the time and money you spend gaming
  • Remember that gaming should be entertainment, not a way to make money
